## ⚔️ STANDARD APPROACH OF HOW WE WILL MOVE FORWARD

- **STEP 1:** KNOW THE BASICS OF THE LANGUAGE THAT YOU WILL BE WORKING ON  
  *(You don't want syntax to halt your preparation.)*

- **STEP 2:** GO FOR THE BASIC PROGRAMS LIKE PRINTING STAR PATTERNS, NUMBER PATTERNS, ETC.  
  *(The goal is to develop a reflexive command over your language.)*

- **STEP 3:** LEARN THE THEORY OF THE DATA STRUCTURE YOU ARE WORKING ON AT THE MOMENT  
  *(Just enough to apply it in problems.)**

- **STEP 4:** LOOK FOR PATTERN-BASED PROBLEM SOLVING IN THAT DATA STRUCTURE  
  *(e.g. Two Pointers and Sliding Window can solve a huge percentage of Array problems.)**

- **STEP 5:** PRACTICE AND COMPETE AS MUCH AS YOU CAN.**
